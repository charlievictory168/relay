Your Data Stays With You

Local-first architecture. Here's exactly what stays on your machine and what doesn't.

On Your Machine
  • Documents & Vector DB (LanceDB)

    All files, embeddings, and indexes

  • System Audio Capture

    Audio processed locally before streaming to STT

  • App Interface & Settings

    All configuration stored locally

Cloud (Minimal, Ephemeral)
  • Small text snippets → OpenAI API

    Your key, B2B zero-retention policy

  • Audio stream → Deepgram API

    Your key, B2B zero-retention policy

Not used for AI model training. All API providers operate under enterprise B2B terms with zero data retention.

Your API Keys (BYOK)

You bring your own OpenAI and Deepgram API keys. Your data is governed by their enterprise B2B policies — zero data retention, never used to train AI models.

No Bots. Ever.

Relay never joins your meeting room. No Zoom bot, no Teams integration, no third-party attendee. Audio is captured at the system level — a local listener on your machine only.

Zero-Retention & Ephemeral

Live transcripts and AI answers exist only in memory during your session — never written to disk. When your session ends, they're gone. Nothing is logged server-side.
